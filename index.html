What is the Average WPM? ▼

The average typing speed is around 40 words per minute (WPM).

What is a CPM? ▼

CPM stands for “characters per minute” and measures the number of characters you can type, including the mistakes.

What is Average CPM? ▼

The average typing speed is around 200 characters per minute (CPM) or 40 words per minute (WPM).
